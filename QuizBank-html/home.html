To Memorize while Typing


Human memory is associated with movement. The act of typing helps your memory. It also helps to improve typing speed.

Hides the correct answer before answering.


If you can easily understand the answer like a normal word book, it will be difficult to exert appropriate stress on the brain. With this app, you can apply moderate stress to the brain by hiding the correct answer.

CountDown Timer


By setting a time limit, you can grasp the playing time and concentrate on your work.

My Flashcard


QuizBank Plus

With the flashcard, you can register only the words you want to focus on and make them your own questions. You can also delete the words you have learned and continue learning only the words that are difficult for you.

Can check the accuracy rate in "My Flashcard" you made.


QuizBank Plus

By checking the accuracy rate, you can see the progress you are memorizing the word little by little. If you have already learned the word, you can delete.
