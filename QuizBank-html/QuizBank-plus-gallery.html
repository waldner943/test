Q & A

Do you accept payment methods other than credit cards?

We accept debit and prepaid cards. However, please note that some issuing companies do not support it.

Do you support bank transfer?

We apologize for the inconvenience, but basically we do not support it because the operation is difficult. However, we will only respond if you can use "half-year payment" for "lump-sum payment," so please contact us. When making inquiries, please include the "address of the invoice" and the "desired date of use".

Is the paid plan available for one month from the date of registration? Or is it until the end of the registration month?

You can use it for one month from the registration date. To be more precise, it will be until the same time on the same day of the month following the registration date. If there is no same day in the following month, it will be until the same time on the last day of the following month.

Are paid plans automatically renewed?

Yes. It will be automatically updated after one month. If you want to cancel, please cancel with sufficient time.

Can I issue a receipt for a paid plan?

Only invoices will be issued. You can view your invoice from "Payment Information" in your account settings page. Please print and use it.
